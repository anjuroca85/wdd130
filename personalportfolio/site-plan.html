Biography Andres Julian Rojas Caballero is from Bucaramanga, Colombia, where he has lived most of his life. He has traveled to several countries, visiting almost all the continents except Australia. Andres comes from a family of six siblings, being the youngest. He served a full-time mission in the South Dakota Rapid City Mission from 2005 to 2007. After his full-time mission, Andres graduated with a three-year associate degree in Electromechanics in March 2008. He is currently married with two beautiful children and loves to serve the Savior and even more observing his family serving the Redeemer. He works in an oil seismic company called Shearwater Geoservices. He does offshore surveys to analyze and study the seabed in search of hydrocarbons. Lately, he has also been involved in Carbon Capture Storage to contribute to the environment. He currently serves in the church as a young men priest quorum adviser. He was a bishop in the Cañaveral ward for almost two years. He knows success is preceded partly by having a great deal of enthusiasm and aptitude for learning. Most importantly, keeping God's commandments. After completing the Pathway Connect program, he did the technical support engineer certificate with Ensign College. His major is a bachelor's degree in Software development. He enjoys Jiu-Jitsu. He loves taking his bicycle into the mountains that surround his hometown.
